The project.

PHEV Coach is a feature of the Renault companion app which aims to help customers with a Plugin Hybrid Vehicle (a PHEV, an hybrid car that can be charged) to make a better use of it, and help them reduce their fuel consumption.

In collaboration with the Car Connected Services team, we designed and implemented this feature, mostly focused on data engineering and visualization.

The key was to provide users with relevant, easy-to-read and consistent data, and go further by creating a dynamic advice system through smart algorithm.
