Staying agile.

I always break down any interface in the tiniest possible bits and pieces.

Bits and pieces here being components and features.

Why? For cherry picking and live prioritisation.

I believe in estimation for organisation. I do. But the best possible estimation is always implement first, and see how long it took.

So for this website, I broke down everything in features and components, to have an overview of the workload and to decide on the fly whether to spend more, less or no time at all on any of those bits or pieces.
