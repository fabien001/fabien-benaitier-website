This is my vision

My role is to gather business strategy, user needs and technical stakes and make it into a realistic Product Vision, which is as clear as necessary to start building it.



My way of doing things is an “around the table” process : I gather stakeholders, taking their data, opinions and feedback as input, and build iteratively a clearer and clearer product vision.

Grandma

What is it that you do for work?

Me

I’m like an architect, but for Digital things.

Grandma

Hmmmmmmmmm...

Me

Say a furniture maker wants to build a physical store.

Like IK...

Yes, but don’t name brands on my website.

So I will help them figure out the needs for this store : the number of potential visitors, parking places, number of entrances, of stories, visitors flows...

And you have a budget?

Sure do, business is key in designing the right thing.

I then design the store to fit all those needs, the budget, the feasability.

But what do you *do* exactly?

The tip of the iceberg is essentially to make renders so that everyone can see what it will look like.

And I make the plans for it to be built.

Grandma

Oh ok, that’s like an architect!

Me

Yep, only I do that for apps, websites... anything digital.

This conversation is almost entirely fictional.

Almost, because I developed this way to explain clearly what I consider my role to be as a Product Designer.

And it’s proven pretty handy,even to explain to other people than my grandma.
